# **Purpose**

- Design BrainLift Coach's daily notification system to achieve 80% completion rates for 14-day expertise sprints by treating user resistance as a feature, not a bug.
  - **In scope:** notification triggers, message framing, resistance patterns.
  - **Out of scope:** content creation, onboarding, payment flows.

# **DOK4 - SPOV**

- **DOK4 SPOV 4.1: The coaching app industry's fear of user fragility has created an epidemic of learned helplessness **- (3.1) proves that apps sending fewer notifications to "respect boundaries" train users to categorize coaching as optional rather than essential, and (3.2) shows users learn more from consistent micro-interactions than content consumption - therefore humans need personalized, constant, and relentless accountability systems that treat comfort as the enemy of growth, with completion rates seemingly tri2pling when apps abandon babysitting for expectation. - **DOK4 SPOV 4.2: BrainLift Coach weaponizes the behavioral transition zone **-\*\* \*\*Most coaching programs systematically fail in the 30-60 day "behavioral transition zone" - too long for streak psychology (which decays after 6-7 weeks) but too short for habit psychology (which requires 4-6 months) - yet are designed as if they're either extended streaks or compressed habits, when in reality they require hybrid engagement mechanics that bridge users from streak dependency to habit formation without the predictable 3-4 week abandonment cliff (that occurs when programs operate between motivation systems) ([3.3](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/33b25c3b9cc7); [3.4](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/102a7b8ae0a3); [3.8](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/3dd3a18855be); [3.11](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/1daffbaccce0)).
- **DOK4 SPOV 4.3: BrainLift Coach exploits the behavioral purgatory gap** - Most coaching programs are criminally designed to fail by existing in the 30-60 day "behavioral purgatory" zone - too long for streak momentum but too short for real habit formation - so BrainLift Coach should either be 14 days maximum (pure streak addiction) or 6+ months minimum (real neurological change), because anything in between is psychological torture that guarantees 3-4 week abandonment regardless of content quality ([3.3](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/33b25c3b9cc7); [3.4](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/102a7b8ae0a3); [3.8](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/3dd3a18855be); [3.11](https://workflowy.com/s/bl-brainlift-coach-e/F0YgTMRKdnglb678#/1daffbaccce0)).
