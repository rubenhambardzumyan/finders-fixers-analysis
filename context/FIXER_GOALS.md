# Fixer Goals - BrainLift Coaching Guidance

## PUR Fixer Goal
**Purpose Statement Coaching**

This Fixer transforms Purpose Statement issues into actionable coaching that helps BrainLift creators define their expertise-building mission.

• Provides laser-focused, 1-2 sentence coaching tied to specific Purpose Statement problems
• Delivers one core action per coaching item to fix clarity, metrics, or scope issues
• Challenges whether the BrainLift should exist if the problem doesn't justify the effort
• Grounds coaching in the actual business context, not abstract concepts
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 110: Pick the BrainLift You Need to Create"
• Makes immediate improvement possible by focusing on the most critical issue first

## EXP Fixer Goal
**Expert Network Coaching**

This Fixer turns Expert section feedback into practical guidance for building a valuable expert network.

• Provides specific coaching for each expert entry that needs improvement
• Focuses on actionable steps to diversify perspectives and avoid echo chambers
• Addresses missing elements: Main Views, Why Follow, agreement/disagreement notes
• Ensures coaching is grounded in the BrainLift's specific problem space
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 121: Building Your Expert Network"
• Helps creators understand which voices matter for their expertise development
• Prioritizes finding experts who challenge the creator's existing worldview

## DOK1 Fixer Goal
**Facts Collection Coaching**

This Fixer converts DOK1 Facts feedback into concrete guidance for extracting valuable, verifiable information.

• Delivers specific coaching for each problematic fact in the Knowledge Tree
• Focuses on making facts specific, verifiable, and properly attributed
• Addresses issues with obvious statements or missing source links
• Provides one clear action to improve fact quality
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 123: Extracting DOK1 Facts and DOK2 Summaries"
• Helps creators understand the boundaries of current knowledge
• Makes the benefit of better fact extraction immediately apparent

## DOK2 Fixer Goal
**Knowledge Synthesis Coaching**

This Fixer transforms DOK2 Summary feedback into actionable steps for better source synthesis.

• Provides targeted coaching for each summary that needs improvement
• Focuses on creating logical connections that explain HOW or WHY
• Addresses objectivity issues and missing support from DOK1 facts
• Delivers one specific action to improve summary quality
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 123: Extracting DOK1 Facts and DOK2 Summaries"
• Helps creators synthesize information into coherent explanations
• Ensures summaries remain objective while building toward insights

## DOK3 Fixer Goal
**Insight Development Coaching**

This Fixer converts DOK3 Insight feedback into guidance for creating valuable, pattern-based insights.

• Provides specific coaching for each insight that lacks depth or grounding
• Focuses on developing practical rules of thumb from multiple sources
• Addresses issues with obvious statements or unsupported claims
• Delivers actionable steps to make insights surprising and valuable
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 200: Deriving DOK3 Insights from Patterns"
• Helps creators find controversial or debatable patterns
• Ensures insights provide foundation for forming SPOVs

## DOK4 Fixer Goal
**Spiky Point of View Coaching**

This Fixer transforms DOK4 SPOV feedback into coaching for developing truly differentiated, actionable viewpoints.

• Provides targeted coaching for each SPOV that lacks controversy or clarity
• Focuses on making SPOVs actionable decision rules, not just opinions
• Addresses issues with common sense statements or lack of grounding in insights
• Delivers specific actions to increase "spikiness" and personal relevance
• Links to lesson: "BrainLift 201: Building DOK4 SPOVs That Stand Out"
• Helps creators develop viewpoints that force readers to take a stance
• Ensures SPOVs guide real operating decisions for solving the BrainLift's problem
